Privacy Policy for Family Tasks Timer

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

Summary: Family Tasks Timer is designed for families. The app does not require an account, does not sell data, does not show third-party ads, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

1. About the App

Family Tasks Timer is a family-friendly app designed to help children and parents manage routines, tasks, and timers. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

2. Information We Collect

Family Tasks Timer is designed to minimize data collection. The app does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Precise location
  • Photos, contacts, or messages

3. Children’s Privacy

Family Tasks Timer may be used by families and children under parent or guardian supervision. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

4. Local App Data

Task names, timer settings, preferences, and routine information may be stored locally on the user’s device to make the app work. This local information is not sold and is not intentionally transmitted to the developer.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on platform or development services such as Google Play Services and Expo. These services may process limited technical information needed for app delivery, diagnostics, security, crash reporting, or platform functionality.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo Privacy Policy

6. Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

Family Tasks Timer does not use third-party advertising and does not sell user data. If analytics or crash reporting is added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what is collected and why.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

8. Data Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect the app and reduce unnecessary data collection. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid collecting sensitive personal information.

9. Data Deletion

Because the app does not require an account, users can delete locally stored app data by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app from their device.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
